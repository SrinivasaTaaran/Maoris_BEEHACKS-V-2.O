In the competitive world of marketing, companies are constantly striving to optimize their strategies and maximize their profitability. One way to achieve this is to use machine learning (ML) to predict profit based on various factors such as research and development (R&D) spend, administration costs, and marketing expenses. By understanding the relationship between these factors and profit, companies can make informed decisions about how to allocate resources and improve their bottom line.

Objective : The objective of this project is to build an ML model that can accurately predict the profit of a marketing company based on R&D spend, administration costs, and marketing expenses. The model will be developed using Python and the Flask framework will be used to deploy the model as a web application.
